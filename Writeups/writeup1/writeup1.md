## Warm-up: SLURM exercise

1. How many slurm job will be submitted? Three slurm jobs will be created, one corresponding to each array. 
2. What is the purpose of the `if` statement? The `if` statement distributes the work over each of the slurm jobs. 
3. What is the expected output in each `*.out` file? Each file will have the data lines assigned to that slurm job.
